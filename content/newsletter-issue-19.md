## ğŸ›¡ï¸ Scam Protection Reminder

AI can help you spot financial scams:

> "I received this message claiming to be from [institution]: [paste message]. Does this look legitimate? What are the warning signs?"

Common red flags AI will help you spot:
- Urgency ("Act now or lose access!")
- Requests for passwords or PINs
- Unusual sender addresses
- Grammar/spelling errors in official communications
- Requests to use unusual payment methods

When in doubt, contact the institution directly using their official number (not from the message).
