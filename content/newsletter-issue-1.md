## üõ†Ô∏è This Week's Tutorial: Your First ChatGPT Conversation

Never used ChatGPT? Here's a 5-minute guide:

**Step 1:** Open your web browser (Chrome, Safari, whatever you use)

**Step 2:** Go to chat.openai.com

**Step 3:** Click "Sign up" and create a free account (you can use your email or Google account)

**Step 4:** You'll see a text box. Type something! Here are three great first messages:

> "I'm new to AI. Can you explain what you are in simple terms?"

> "Help me write a thank-you note to my neighbour who looked after my cat last week."

> "I'm planning a weekend trip to [your city]. What should I see and do?"

**Step 5:** Read the response. If you want more detail, just ask! Say "Can you explain that more simply?" or "Give me more options."

**That's it.** You're now using AI. Congratulations!

**Pro tip:** Be specific. Instead of "Write me a letter," try "Write a polite letter to my insurance company explaining that my claim for the roof damage from the January storm hasn't been processed after 3 weeks."
