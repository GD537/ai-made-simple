### Australian Healthcare AI Guidelines Released

The Australian Medical Association released new guidelines on AI in healthcare this week. Key points:

**What doctors can now use AI for:**
- Assisting with image analysis (X-rays, scans)
- Administrative tasks (notes, referrals)
- Research and staying current on treatments
- Patient communication support

**What AI should NOT be used for:**
- Making diagnoses without human oversight
- Replacing the doctor-patient relationship
- Making treatment decisions autonomously

**What this means for you:** If your doctor mentions using AI assistance, that's normal and increasingly common. They're still making the decisions â€” AI is just another tool in their kit.
