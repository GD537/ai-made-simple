Planning a trip used to mean hours of research across dozens of websites. Now it takes 5 minutes.

### The Complete Trip Planner

Open ChatGPT or Google Gemini and try this:

> "Plan a 7-day trip to New Zealand's South Island for a couple in our 60s. We love scenery, wine, and easy walks (no strenuous hiking). Budget is $5,000 AUD total excluding flights. We're from Perth, Australia. We prefer boutique hotels over big chains. We have a rental car."

Watch what happens. You'll get:
- Day-by-day itinerary
- Hotel recommendations (with price estimates)
- Restaurant suggestions
- Driving distances between stops
- Activity recommendations matched to your preferences

### Making It Better

After the first response, refine it:

> "Day 3 looks too busy. Can you make it more relaxed? Also, I forgot to mention â€” my wife is vegetarian."

> "Add a backup plan for Day 5 in case of rain."

> "What should I pack for this trip in March?"

> "What's the best travel insurance for over-60s going to NZ?"

Each follow-up makes the plan more personalised.

### The Restaurant Finder

Already on holiday? Try:

> "I'm in Queenstown, New Zealand. Recommend 3 restaurants for dinner tonight. One nice, one casual, one that does great seafood. Budget for two is around $100-150 NZD."

### The Packing List

> "Create a packing list for 7 days in New Zealand in March. I'm a 65-year-old woman who prefers comfortable, smart-casual clothing. Include toiletries I might forget."

### Pro Tips:
- **Be specific about YOUR preferences.** AI can't read your mind â€” tell it what you like.
- **Mention health considerations.** "I have knee problems" changes walk recommendations.
- **Ask about local customs.** "What should I know about tipping in New Zealand?"
- **Get it to compare options.** "Should I do Milford Sound or Doubtful Sound? Compare them for a 60+ couple."
