### Getting Started with Genealogy AI

You don't need to be a historian. AI can help anyone explore their family story.

**Step 1: Start with what you know**

Open ChatGPT and try:

> "I want to research my family history. My grandfather was born in [town/country] around [year]. His name was [name]. His wife was [name] from [town]. Can you suggest where I should start looking for records? What kind of documents might exist from that era and location?"

AI gives you a research roadmap tailored to your family's specific origins.

**Step 2: Understand historical records**

> "What kind of genealogy records exist for people who lived in [country] in the [decade]? Where can I access these records from Australia? Are any free?"

AI knows which archives, databases, and websites hold records from specific regions and time periods.

**Step 3: Decode old documents**

Found an old document but can't read it? Take a photo and describe it:

> "I found my grandmother's marriage certificate from 1948 in Queensland. It mentions 'banns' and 'solemnised.' Can you explain what all the different sections of an old Australian marriage certificate mean?"

**Step 4: Restore family photos**

Got faded, damaged, or black-and-white photos?

- **MyHeritage**: Upload a photo â†’ Click "Enhance" or "Colorize" (free to try)
- **Google Photos**: Use "Magic Eraser" to fix blemishes
- **Remini** (app): Enhances face details in old group photos

> "I have a faded black-and-white photo of my parents' wedding from 1962. What are the best free AI tools to restore, enhance, and colorize it?"

**Step 5: Write the family story**

This is where AI truly shines:

> "I'm writing my family history for my grandchildren. Here are the key facts I know about my grandparents: [paste your notes]. Can you help me turn these facts into an engaging narrative that a teenager would enjoy reading? Include historical context about what was happening in [country] at that time."

AI transforms dry facts into a compelling family story.

## ğŸ“§ Reader Question

*"I found old letters written in German from my great-grandparents. Can AI translate them?"* â€” Keith, 73

Keith, absolutely! Here are your options:

1. **Google Lens** (free): Point your phone camera at the letter. Tap "Translate." It translates in real-time, right over the text.

2. **ChatGPT**: Type or paste the text and say "Translate this German text to English. It's from a personal letter written around 1920, so some terms may be old-fashioned."

3. **Google Translate** (free): Type or paste the text for translation.

For old handwritten German (especially Kurrent script), Google Lens may struggle. Try taking a clear photo, then asking ChatGPT: "This is a scan of a handwritten German letter from 1920. Can you help me read and translate it?" and describe what you see.
