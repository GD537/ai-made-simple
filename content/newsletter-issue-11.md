### AI Can Now Pass Medical Exams â€” But Should It Be Your Doctor?

This week, researchers announced that GPT-5 scored in the 90th percentile on medical licensing exams â€” better than most human medical students.

**What this means:**
AI is extraordinarily good at medical knowledge. It can recall symptoms, drug interactions, and treatment protocols with near-perfect accuracy.

**What this DOESN'T mean:**
AI should not be your doctor. Here's why:

1. **Context matters.** AI doesn't know you've been stressed about your daughter's wedding for six months, which might explain your symptoms better than any textbook.

2. **Physical examination.** AI can't feel that your lymph nodes are swollen or notice that you're avoiding eye contact when discussing certain symptoms.

3. **Nuance.** Medicine is full of "usually... but sometimes." Human doctors develop intuition. AI follows probabilities.

4. **Accountability.** Who's responsible when AI makes a mistake? Your doctor takes an oath.

**The smart approach:** Use AI to understand, research, and prepare. Use humans to diagnose, decide, and care.

Let's flip the script. Instead of worrying about what AI can do, let's appreciate what it can't.

### 1. Genuine Human Connection

AI can simulate conversation. It can say "I'm sorry you're going through that." But it doesn't feel empathy. It predicts the words that typically follow your message.

**Your advantage:** When you console a friend, celebrate with family, or comfort a grandchild â€” that's irreplaceable human connection.

### 2. Physical Presence and Touch

AI can't hold your hand during a difficult moment. It can't hug you when you're sad. It can't be physically present.

**This matters because:** So much of human life happens through presence â€” a shared meal, a walk together, sitting in comfortable silence.

### 3. Lived Experience and Wisdom

AI trained on text from the internet. You lived a life. You raised children. You navigated loss. You built relationships. You made mistakes and learned from them.

**Your wisdom is unique.** When your grandchild asks for advice, they don't want GPT-4's probability-weighted response. They want YOUR experience, YOUR values, YOUR story.

### 4. Creativity from the Soul

AI can generate art, music, and writing. But it's combining patterns it learned. It doesn't dream. It doesn't feel the urge to express something deep inside.

**True creativity:** That poem you write for your partner. The garden you design. The story only you can tell. These come from a place AI can't access.

### 5. Moral Judgment and Values

AI can tell you what people generally consider right or wrong. It can't have values. It doesn't believe in anything. It doesn't stand for anything.

**You do.** Your decisions are informed by a lifetime of values, beliefs, and principles. AI optimises for likely responses. You act on what you believe is right.

### 6. Unpredictability and Serendipity

AI is fundamentally about prediction â€” finding the most likely next word, response, or outcome. But some of life's best moments are unpredictable.

**Falling in love, unexpected friendships, sudden insights, the joy of a surprise** â€” these emerge from the beautiful chaos of being human. AI can't stumble into discoveries the way we do.
