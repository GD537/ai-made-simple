### Hospital AI: How Australian Hospitals Are Using AI

Several Australian hospitals made news this week for their expanding use of AI:

**What's happening:**
- **Radiology**: AI helps doctors spot issues in X-rays, CT scans, and MRIs faster. At some hospitals, AI flags potential problems for human doctors to review. This means faster diagnosis.
- **Emergency Departments**: AI triaging systems help prioritise patients based on symptoms, reducing wait times.
- **Pathology**: AI analyses blood tests and tissue samples with high accuracy.
- **Mental Health**: AI chatbots provide initial screening and crisis support between appointments.

**What this means for you as a patient:**
- AI is assisting your doctors, not replacing them
- You may notice faster test results
- Your doctor still makes all final decisions
- It's completely safe ‚Äî AI is a tool your medical team uses, like a stethoscope

**Should you worry?** No. AI in healthcare is heavily regulated and always supervised by human doctors. It's making healthcare better, not replacing the human element.

### For Grandparents: Connect with Grandkids

**1. Understand Their World**

> "My grandson is 14 and loves Minecraft and TikTok. I want to understand what he's interested in so we can have better conversations. Can you explain these things to me in a way that helps me connect with him?"

AI helps you understand references, games, and trends without needing to ask your grandchild (who might not explain it well).

**2. Plan Activities Together**

> "Suggest 5 activities a 70-year-old grandmother and a 12-year-old granddaughter can do together this weekend. We live in [city]. Budget $50."

AI finds activities that bridge the age gap.

**3. Write Meaningful Messages**

> "Help me write a letter to my 18-year-old grandson who's starting university next month. I want to share some life wisdom but not sound preachy. I want him to keep it."

AI helps you find the right words for important moments.

**4. Create a Family Cookbook**

> "Help me organise my family recipes into a cookbook format. I have about 30 recipes I want to preserve for my grandchildren. Create a nice introduction and suggest how to organise them by category."

Preserve family traditions in a format grandchildren will actually use.

**5. Record Family Stories**

> "I want to record my life stories for my grandchildren. Can you give me a list of 20 questions I should answer? Things about my childhood, my career, our family history, and life lessons I want to pass on."

AI creates interview questions that draw out the stories that matter most.

### For Grandkids: Help Your Grandparents

**1. Set Them Up**

Spend 15 minutes:
- Go to chat.openai.com on their phone/computer
- Create a free account for them
- Show them how to type a question
- Show them one useful thing (writing an email, explaining something)

**2. Create Their "Starter Prompts"**

Save these in their phone's Notes app:
- "Write a polite email about [situation]"
- "Explain this in simple terms: [paste]"
- "I have [ingredients]. What can I cook?"
- "Help me write a message to [person] for [occasion]"

**3. Be Patient**

They taught you to tie your shoes. You can teach them to type a question. Same patience applies.
