## THREAD 4: What AI can't do (and why that matters)

üßµ "Everyone talks about what AI CAN do. Let's talk about what it CAN'T.

1/ AI can't feel emotions.
It can recognise emotion in text. It can generate emotional text.
But it doesn't feel joy, sadness, or empathy.
When ChatGPT says 'I'm sorry you're going through that' ‚Äî it's pattern matching, not compassion.

2/ AI can't guarantee truth.
It predicts likely text based on patterns. Sometimes those patterns lead to confident, well-written nonsense.
This is called 'hallucination.' It's the #1 thing to remember.
Always verify important information.

3/ AI can't understand your personal context.
It doesn't know your medical history. Your family dynamics. Your financial situation.
It gives GENERAL advice based on patterns.
For PERSONAL decisions ‚Äî talk to real humans who know you.

4/ AI can't replace human connection.
It can simulate conversation. It can be comforting.
But it's not a friend, therapist, or partner.
Use it as a TOOL, not a relationship.

5/ AI can't predict the future.
Despite what crypto bros tell you, AI can't reliably predict stock markets, elections, or sports outcomes.
Anyone selling AI 'predictions' is selling snake oil.

6/ AI can't be creative (truly).
It remixes existing patterns brilliantly.
But genuine creativity ‚Äî the kind that changes the world ‚Äî comes from human experience, emotion, and intuition.
AI is a powerful TOOL for creative people. Not a replacement for them.

WHY THIS MATTERS:
Understanding AI's limits makes you a BETTER user of AI.
You'll know when to trust it. When to verify it. When to ignore it.

That's wisdom. And no AI can teach you that.
