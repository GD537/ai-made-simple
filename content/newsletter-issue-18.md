### Learning Any Topic

**The magic prompt:**

> "I want to learn about [topic]. I'm [age/background], and I learn best with [practical examples/visual explanations/step-by-step guides]. Create a learning plan that takes me from complete beginner to comfortable understanding over [timeframe]. Start with the first lesson."

**Example:**

> "I want to learn about the stock market. I'm 67, retired, and have some savings I'd like to understand better. I learn best with real-world examples. Create a learning plan that takes me from complete beginner to comfortable understanding over 4 weeks. Start with the first lesson."

AI creates a personalized curriculum just for you.

### Learning Languages

> "I want to practice conversational Spanish. I'm a beginner. Let's have a simple conversation about [topic]. Correct my mistakes gently and explain why. Keep it at my level."

Then just... have a conversation. AI is infinitely patient with your mistakes.

### Understanding Historical Events

> "Explain [historical event] like I'm a curious adult who wants to understand it deeply. Include the context, the key players, why it matters today, and what's most commonly misunderstood about it."

Better than most textbooks.

### Learning Creative Skills

> "I want to learn watercolor painting. I'm a complete beginner with no art background. Give me my first lesson: what materials do I need, and what should I try in my first 30 minutes?"

AI can't paint for you â€” but it can teach you step by step.
