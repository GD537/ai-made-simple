**2. Preparing for Appointments**
> "I'm seeing my doctor about persistent knee pain that's worse in the morning. What questions should I ask? I'm 67 years old."

> "I'm 65, have mild arthritis in both knees, and want to stay active. Suggest a gentle weekly exercise plan I can do at home with no equipment."

**5. Nutrition Questions**
> "I have Type 2 diabetes and high blood pressure. Create a simple weekly meal plan that's good for both conditions. I live alone and cook basic meals."

### The Smart Way: AI + Doctor = Best

1. **Before** your appointment: Use AI to prepare questions
2. **During**: Focus on your doctor's expertise
3. **After**: Use AI to understand what your doctor told you
