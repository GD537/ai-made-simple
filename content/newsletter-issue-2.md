## üõ†Ô∏è This Week's Tutorial: AI Meal Planning

Tired of the "What's for dinner?" question? AI can help. Here's how:

### The Fridge Clean-Out Recipe

Open ChatGPT and type:

> "I have chicken thighs, potatoes, onions, garlic, and olive oil. I want something easy for dinner that takes under 45 minutes. I'm cooking for two."

You'll get a complete recipe ‚Äî ingredients, steps, cooking times. It's like having a chef friend on call.

### The Weekly Meal Plan

Try this:

> "Create a weekly meal plan for two adults in their 60s. We prefer Mediterranean-style food, eat fish twice a week, and want each meal to cost under $15 to make. Include a shopping list organised by supermarket section."

That last part ‚Äî "organised by supermarket section" ‚Äî is the secret. AI will group your list by produce, dairy, meat, pantry staples. No more backtracking through aisles.

### The Dietary Needs Planner

> "I need a week of meals that are low sodium, no red meat, and diabetes-friendly. I live alone and don't want complicated recipes. I have a slow cooker."

AI handles dietary restrictions brilliantly because you can be very specific about YOUR situation.

### Pro Tips:
- **Tell AI your cooking skill level.** "I'm a beginner cook" changes everything.
- **Mention your equipment.** "I only have a stovetop and microwave" prevents suggestions you can't use.
- **Ask for substitutions.** "What can I use instead of cream?" and it'll suggest alternatives.
- **Say "simpler"** if a recipe looks too complex. AI will simplify it.

## üí° Quick Tip
